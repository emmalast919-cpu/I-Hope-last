Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Aimo ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord music bot. By using Aimo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you interact with Aimo, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Discord User IDs — to identify users for features like playlists, Last.fm linking, and premium access
  • Discord Server IDs — to store per-server settings such as DJ roles, volume preferences, and autoplay configuration
  • Command Usage Data — to monitor bot health, detect abuse, and improve features
  • Last.fm Usernames — only when you voluntarily link your Last.fm account via the /lastfm command
  • Playlist Data — track names, URLs, and playlist names you save through Aimo's playlist commands

We do not collect message content, voice audio, personal email addresses, or payment information directly.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve Aimo's services:

  • Delivering music playback, queue management, and playlist features
  • Remembering per-server and per-user preferences
  • Enabling Last.fm scrobbling and recommendations
  • Managing premium feature access
  • Detecting and preventing abuse or service disruption

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secured servers and is not sold to third parties. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Stored data is limited to what is necessary for bot functionality.

Third-Party Services

Aimo integrates with third-party platforms to provide its features:

  • YouTube & SoundCloud — for audio streaming (subject to their respective terms)
  • Last.fm — for scrobbling and music recommendations (subject to Last.fm's Privacy Policy)
  • Discord — all interactions occur through Discord's platform (subject to Discord's Privacy Policy)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide Aimo's features. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us through our support server. Upon request, we will remove your User ID, linked accounts, and any associated playlist or preference data within a reasonable timeframe.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please reach out through our support server.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced in our support server. Continued use of Aimo after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
